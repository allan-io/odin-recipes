Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish which if done correctly is an absolute masterpiece.

I will go through a nice and easy recipe which will leave your guests wanting more!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna Sheets
  • 1 Pound Ground Beef
  • 1 Chopped Onion
  • 2 Chooped Garlic Cloves
  • 1 Canned Tomato
  • 1/2 Pound Mozzarella Cheese
  • 1 cup Ricotta

Steps

  1. cook pasta in boiling water
  2. cook onion and garlic in skillet
  3. cook ground beef in skillet
  4. add canned tomatoes and cook for 10 minutes
  5. layer pasta sheets with sauce and cheese
  6. finish in hot oven for 10 minutes until cheese is melted
